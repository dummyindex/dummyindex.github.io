#### **Virtual Chat**

I hold a **30-minute virtual chat each week** for current Ph.D. students. If you are facing Ph.D.-related difficulties, especially **advisor–trainee tensions**, I am happy to talk them through with you and share what I learned from my own past experience. If this would be helpful, please reach out by [email](mailto:keniauthor@gmail.com).
